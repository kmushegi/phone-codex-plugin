# Phone Agent Terms of Service

Effective date: April 27, 2026

Phone Agent lets users direct a hosted phone assistant to place outbound calls after explicit approval.
The hosted service is voice-only and does not provide SMS, MMS, short-code, or text-message campaign features.

## User responsibilities

Users are responsible for:

- Providing accurate call instructions.
- Confirming the destination, objective, constraints, and any sensitive details before a call is placed.
- Using Phone Agent only for lawful, non-abusive calls.
- Complying with applicable recording, consent, telemarketing, healthcare, financial, and appointment-booking rules.
- Reviewing call results before relying on them or adding anything to a calendar.

## Prohibited use

Users may not use Phone Agent to harass, deceive, impersonate without authorization, spam, commit fraud, collect sensitive information without consent, place emergency calls, or perform regulated activities without required authorization.

## Calls and outcomes

Phone Agent may fail to connect, may be misunderstood by called parties, or may produce incomplete results. Users should verify important reservations, appointments, cancellations, or account-impacting outcomes directly when accuracy matters.

## Access and credentials

API keys are scoped to individual hosted users. Users must keep keys confidential and may not share credentials with unauthorized parties. Access may be suspended or revoked for abuse, security risk, or operational reasons.

## Availability

The hosted service is provided as-is and may change, pause, or become unavailable. The operator may update these terms as the service evolves.

## Contact

For support or terms questions, contact the operator of the hosted Phone Agent deployment.
